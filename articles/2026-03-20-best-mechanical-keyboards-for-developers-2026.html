1. Cherry MX Red

The Cherry MX Red is a popular choice among developers for its smooth, fast typing experience. The red switches are known for their linear design, which provides a consistent feel and response without the tactile bump found in other types of switches. This makes the Cherry MX Red an excellent choice for fast-paced typing, such as in coding environments. The keyboard is also very durable, which is crucial for the high volume of typing developers often do.

            // Example of fast typing with Cherry MX Red

                System.out.println(i);
            }

The keyboard is also available in a full-sized 104-key layout, which is perfect for those who need more space for their hands. It's also compatible with various operating systems, including Windows, macOS, and Linux.
